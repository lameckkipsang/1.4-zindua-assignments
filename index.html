Our Story

This is the Breakfast Cafe. It first opened up on a regular Monday in the year 2021. It started as a way to earn a little cash to get by, but with every taste of the coffee we offered, it turned into a place where people would come to converse, read a magazine, or work on their remote jobs. It brought people together over good coffee. It eventually turned into a community pride—people have even proposed here! All because of good, healthy coffee.
